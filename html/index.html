School life at Inter-Lakes

« Experiencing the American school system has given me a completely new opinion on our own way of teaching in Italy.
For better or for worse, I came to appreciate our education more, but I also still see immense areas for improvement.
We should learn from other countries, such as the United States, without obviously losing our distinctive spirit. »

Meredith village

« It has been great to live in this city for so long.
Full of life even in the most difficult times, what struck me was its people, and how they changed their attitude depending on the season.
Extremely touristy in the summer season, but I loved it in the coldest moments. »

New Hampshire the granite state

« American culture was curiously more interesting than expected.
After the first few months I finally realized that what they lack in ancient history, they make up for with great pride for their legacy and present character on the world scene. »

My second family

« These two people saved me in one of the most difficult moments of my life, giving me new energy.
Their passion for everything they do, the love they honestly show to their aniamls, and their life philosophy are true morals I easily came to love. »

WEP Italy

« The exchange year changes you in infinite ways, something that no word alone can describe.
I have been lucky at numerous points in my journey and have learned a lot, through suffering and accomplishments. »
