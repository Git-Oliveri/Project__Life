⚖ Concord, the capital ⚖

⇻ Concord State House

Visiting the State House gave me the opportunity to experience the history of New Hampshire firsthand, with relics from old times, and interesting stories.
Shout-out to the exquisite staff who showed us everything we wanted to know, and more.

⇻ The landscapes

New Hampshire can offer a lot when approached with the right mindset. Complaining about the cold weather, or the constant wind won't get you far here.
Instead, we chose to focus on the beautiful views and the full nature of this state. With 80% of the land covered by forests, there was plenty of outdoor space to discover.

⇻ Bonus round, Boston

Fortunately for me I had a native (Bill) from Boston who walked me through the city telling stories and insights of his earlier days at MIT.
We saw the Celtics playing at the Boston Garden, which was both a show and a sporting competition, very different from our concept of game day.
Hope to come back here someday.
